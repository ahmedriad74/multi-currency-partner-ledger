Extension of Ukrainian localization

This module is a part of the Self-ERP suite and is required by many other modules. It extends the l10n_ua module to provide additional internationalization functionality specific to the Ukrainian market. The module includes the following features:

  • Adds correct spelling of Ukrainian currency in hryvnias when displaying sums.
  • Provides a basic report layout for financial statements.
  • Enhances the post address view to conform with local regulations.
  • Modifies the company model to comply with Ukrainian legal requirements.
  • Adds statuses of regulated reports
  • Adds an additional filter "by account" to accounting reports
  • Adds the "Accounting date" field to the Stock picking - specifying the date on which the journal entry should be created
  • Adds a directory "Tax inspections" for Ukraine
  • Adds additional fields to the Company card
  • Adds a list of areas to the "Area directory for Ukraine"

By installing this module, users can benefit from these additional features that are not available in the standard l10n_ua module, allowing for greater accuracy and compliance with Ukrainian regulations in their financial and accounting processes.
